Thank you for installing the host software for our cameras, including the ThorCam imaging application.

If you wish to run ThorCam, you can exit this page and run it from the start menu (Press the Start button, then select Thorlabs -> Scientific Imaging -> ThorCam).

Save Time. Identify Your Camera Family!

Regardless of your purchase or driver selection, you will see folders and files for both our camera families. Though both run with ThorCam, their underlying drivers, SDK, and third-party support are very different and will not interoperate! Save time by identifying your camera family and selecting only the support required for your camera.

Scientific Cameras have a large black and red aluminum case. The Scientific Camera family has its own drivers (USB 3.0, Camera Link, and Gigabit Ethernet) and SDK.

Scientific Cameras

 

 Scientific Camera files are located in the following folders

-        Documentation: C:\Program Files\Thorlabs\Scientific Imaging\documentation\Scientific

-        Third-Party Support, SDK, and Examples: C:\Program Files\Thorlabs\Scientific Imaging\Scientific Camera Support

 

DCC- or DCU- (also referred to as DCx) Compact USB Cameras are small with either a black/stainless steel or grey plastic case, depending on the model. The DCx Camera family has its own USB drivers, SDK, and third-party support.

DCU/DCC cameras

 

DCx Camera files are located in the following folders

-        Documentation: C:\Program Files\Thorlabs\Scientific Imaging\documentation\DCx

-        Third-Party Support, SDK, Interfaces, and Examples: C:\Program Files\Thorlabs\Scientific Imaging\DCx Camera Support
